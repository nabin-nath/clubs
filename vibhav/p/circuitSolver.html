VIBHAV - Circuit Solver
Images
Description:

Objective:

To solve a circuit by scanning its image. We need to click a picture of an electronic circuit and it will solve the circuit for us.

Abstract:

The proposed model consists of all possible components of a diagram recognition system, such as segmentation, feature extraction, classification and redrawing and repositioning. Then we use this circuit for simulation purpose by substituting values for each component to generate output waveforms/characteristics graph.

Execution:

First scan the hand-drawn circuit image and then with the help of our model, the corresponding LtSpice Schematic will be created. This schematic can be simulated in the LtSpice and required knowledge of the circuit can be obtained.

Applications:

• The project can prove to be of great use for domestic workers with less knowledge of complex circuits. They can use it reliably in their fieldwork and can be more useful.
• The project can open new doors in creative minds and with further innovations it can be of immense use in education, technology industry and many more fields.
